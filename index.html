About Me

Hey there! My name is Samuel but I go by the nickname vh8t. It may seem like a 4 random characters, and you are completely right!It's a quirky identifier I came up with by generating a 4-character string. While it lacks any profound meaning, it's become a part of my online presence.

I primarly code in Go, C++ and Python but recently I started learning Nim and Assembly. My go-to coding editor must definitelly be NeoVim although I also use JetBrains IDEs. I post most of my code on GitHub so you migh as well check it out :)

In my free time I like to play basketball or go to the gym, one of my more unusual hobbies is speedcubing. I love the challange of solving Rubik's Cubes, particularly 3x3 and 2x2 variants. My personal best times are 23.685 seconds on 3x3 and 3.961 seconds for the 2x2.

In all aspects of life, I appreciate simplicity. Whether it's in coding, solving puzzles, or navigating everyday challenges, I find beauty in elegant solutions and streamlined processes. Life is complex enough as it is, and embracing simplicity helps me stay grounded and focused on what truly matters.
